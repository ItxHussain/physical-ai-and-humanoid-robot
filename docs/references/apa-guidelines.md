# APA Citation Guidelines

This document provides guidelines for implementing APA citations throughout the Physical AI Modules Book.

## Basic APA Format

### Journal Articles
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical*, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy

### Books
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work*. Publisher.

### Conference Papers
Author, A. A. (Year, Month). Title of paper. In *Proceedings of conference* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher. URL

## Implementation Notes

- Citations should be placed directly after the statement they support
